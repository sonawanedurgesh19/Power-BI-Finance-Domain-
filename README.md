Project Title :
Bank Loan Performance Analysis & Risk Monitoring System    

Short Description :
A comprehensive three-part Power BI reporting solution designed to monitor a bank’s lending operations. The project tracks loan application volumes, funding disbursements, and repayment performance, while specifically segmenting data to distinguish between "Good" and "Bad" loan portfolios for risk assessment.    

Problem Statement :
The lending institution requires a data-driven method to:
Monitor Core Metrics: Calculate and track Total Loan Applications, Total Funded Amount, and Total Amount Received on a Month-to-Date (MTD) and Month-over-Month (MoM) basis.    
Assess Financial Health: Evaluate the lending cost and borrower risk through Average Interest Rates and Average Debt-to-Income (DTI) ratios.    
Differentiate Portfolio Quality: Classify loans as "Good" or "Bad" to understand the percentage of healthy vs. non-performing assets.    
Identify Trends: Pinpoint seasonal lending patterns, regional disparities, and the impact of borrower attributes (like employment length and home ownership) on loan performance.    

Goal of the Dashboard :
The primary objective is to provide a one-stop solution for users to gain a holistic snapshot of critical loan-related metrics. It aims to empower stakeholders to make informed, data-driven decisions regarding lending strategies and risk management through three specialized views: Summary, Overview, and Details.    

Walkthrough of Key Visuals :

1. Summary Dashboard :
KPI Cards: Displaying Total Applications (38.6K), Total Funded Amount ($435.8M), and Total Amount Received ($473.1M) with MoM and MTD growth indicators.    
Good vs. Bad Loan Gauges: Visual indicators showing that 86.2% of loans are "Good" and 13.8% are "Bad."    
Loan Status Grid: A tabular breakdown of metrics (Interest Rate, DTI, etc.) categorized by status: Current, Fully Paid, and Charged Off.    

2. Overview Dashboard :
Monthly Trend (Line Chart): Tracks the "Total Funded Amount" from Jan ($25M) to Dec ($54M), showing a steady upward trend throughout the year.    
Regional Analysis (Filled Map): Identifies lending activity and disparities across different states.    
Employee Length (Bar Chart): Shows that borrowers with 10+ years of experience represent the largest funded segment ($116M).    
Loan Purpose (Bar Chart): Breaks down disbursements by reason, with Debt Consolidation being the primary driver.    

3. Details Dashboard :
Consolidated Grid: A granular table providing raw data on individual loans, including ID, purpose, home ownership, grade, sub-grade, and specific financial terms.    

Business Impact :
Risk Mitigation: By monitoring the "Bad Loan" percentage (currently 13.8%) and Average DTI (13.33%), the bank can adjust lending criteria to reduce defaults.    
Operational Efficiency: The "Details Dashboard" saves time by consolidating disparate loan data into a single, searchable interface for loan officers.    
Strategic Growth: Identifying that "Debt Consolidation" is the leading loan purpose (62.66% of funded amount) allows the bank to tailor marketing and products to this high-demand segment.    

Key Insights :
Growth Momentum: Total Loan Applications grew by 6.9% MoM, while the Total Amount Received saw a significant 15.8% MoM increase.    
Portfolio Health: The vast majority of the portfolio (86.2%) consists of "Good Loans," which have returned approximately $435.8M to the bank.    
Interest Rate Dynamics: "Charged Off" (Bad) loans carry a higher average interest rate (13.88%) compared to "Fully Paid" loans (11.64%), reflecting the risk-based pricing strategy.    
Borrower Profiles: Borrowers with Mortgages and Renters hold the largest shares of funded amounts ($219.3M and $185.8M respectively), suggesting higher lending engagement compared to homeowners.
